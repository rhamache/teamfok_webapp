User Manual

Home Page

  • Once you entered the web application via /proj1/ you are greeted by 3 options. Home, Login and Help. 
    • Home and Help button will be persistent in the top left throughout the whole webapplication. you will be able to navigate back to home or help from anywhere. Home basically takes you back to /proj/ page. Help invokes a series of tutorials and guides to help you use the web app.
    • To login or register for an account you would press Login on this page.

Login Page

  • On this page you will be promted to enter a username and password in order to logon. If you have an account simply enter your credentials you will then be redirected to the "Display page". 
    • If you have entered a typo or wrong password, you will be redirected to a page promting you to try again. Simply press "Try again" to return to the login screen.
  • Otherwise you would have to register for a new account. To do this, simply click on "Register here" underneath the logon form
  • Once logged on , on the top left, you will now see more options then before. The "Login" will then change to "Logout". At anytime, when you feel like you are done looking at photos, press "Logout" which will redirect you to a "Logout Page" which is essentailly a home page with the message of succesfully logged out.

Registration Page

  • On this page, you are presented with a registration form. Simply enter each field and press sumit.
    • The username you enter should contain no spaces. For security reasons, your password should be non trivial. First name and last name should only consist of one word each. Email field should contain 1 and only 1 "@" sign with a proper domain and lastly phone number should be eneter in 10 digits only with no hyphens. (e.g. 5871234567) If any one field is not valid, you will remain on the same page and continue the registration.
  • Upon succession, you will be redirected to a page with different optsions with your newly created credentials or your original login credentials. the menu consist of Home, Logout (previously Login), Search, Groups, Upload, Display, Data Analysis (Admin only) and Help.

Search page

  • This page is used to search the database with specific requirements. For example if you would like to search any photos with the location tag of "Outter Space", then in the text box, type in "outter space", proceed to press "Excecute" at the bottom of the form. You will see all photos with tags of "outter" or "space" in any 3 fields (subject, location or description).
  • To execute a search with date time constrait, enter into the date fields following this convetion, dd-MON-yyyy. For example for search date September 11th, 2001 to September 11th 2011, you would enter into the first field 11-SEP-2001 and in the second field 11-SEP-2011. then proceed to press execute. Here are all the month abbreviations.
    • Janurary - JAN
    • Feburary - FEB
    • March - MAR
    • April - APR
    • May - MAY
    • June - JUN
    • July - JLY
    • August - AUG
    • September - SEP
    • October - OCT
    • November - NOV
    • December - DEC

Groups page

  • You could also create groups and form a private community for photo sharing. To do this, you will need to access the "Groups page" .
  • In the  "Groups page", you will be able to see groups you have created or groups your friends have invited you into.
  • To create a group, click on "create group". Type in a group name and press submit.
  • To delete a group you have created or join, simply press the "Delete" button underneath the groups you want to delete or leave. 

Upload Page

  • You use this page to upload files. To choose a file or files to upload, click the "Choose Files" button. A OS window will pop up and you can use the normal conventional single select or multi select with the control key to choose files to upload.
  • Then proceed to type in a subject, place and description in the corresponding fields. 
  • Then lastly, you can choose privacy of the image/images you are uploading. Press on the privacy drop down menu and you can choose the image/images to be either public, private or private to a specific group that you belong to. 
  • When all fields are filled in, press the "Upload" button. Upon succession, you will be greeted with the same upload page with empty fields and a "Upload OK!" message displayed on screen. You can navigate to anywhere from this point on using the menu.
  • Another thing you could do here on the "Upload page" is to view all your uploaded photos. Press on "My Uploaded Photos"  and you will be redirected to another page with all your uploaded images.

Display page

  • On this page you are, if the database is populated, greeted with beautiful photos in your database.
  • To view the original image (original resolution) simply click on the thumbnail of the image.
  • Beside each photo you can see all the info of the image. That includes the Owner/Uploader of the image, subject, place, date of upload, description, hits and group it belongs to.
  • If you are the owner/uploader of the image, at the end of all those info above, there will be a "Delete" button, this allows you to delete the photo right away.

Data Analysis page (Admin Only)

  • The "Data Analysis" option will only appear on the top left menu if you are logged in as Administrator
  • Similar to "Search Page", Administrator can specify and choose getting image count for either users or subject or both. The date is simply a calender plugin. Press the far right of the input field to get a drop down menu of a calender and select the dates desired.
  • And lastly, choose the partition of the result from the radio button of either yearly, monthly or weekly. Then proceed to press Generate.

Help Page

  • As mentioned above the help page is avalible to you at all times.
