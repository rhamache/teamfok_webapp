User Manual

Home Page

  • Once you entered the web application via /proj1/ you are greeted by 3 options. Home, Login and Help. 
    • Home and Help button will be persistent in the top left throughout the whole webapplication. you will be able to navigate back to home or help from anywhere. Home basically takes you back to /proj/ page. Help invokes a series of tutorials and guides to help you use the web app.
    • To login or register for an account you would press Login on this page.

Login Page

  • On this page you will be promted to enter a username and password in order to logon. If you have an account simply enter your credentials you will then be redirected to the "Display page". 
    • If you have entered a typo or wrong password, you will be redirected to a page promting you to try again. Simply press "Try again" to return to the login screen.
  • Otherwise you would have to register for a new account. To do this, simply click on "Register here" underneath the logon form

Registration Page

  • On this page, you are presented with a registration form. Simply enter each field and press sumit.
    • The username you enter should contain no spaces. For security reasons, your password should be non trivial. First name and last name should only consist of one word each. Email field should contain 1 and only 1 "@" sign with a proper domain and lastly phone number should be eneter in 10 digits only with no hyphens. (e.g. 5871234567) If any one field is not valid, you will be redirected to the same page and continue the registration.
  • Upon succession, you will be redirected to the the "Display page" logged on with your newly created credentials.

Display page

  • On this page you are, if the database is populated, greeted with beautiful photos in your database. On the top left, you will now see more options then before. Aside from Home, Help, you now also have Logout (before it is the Login button), Upload, Display Analysis. These buttons will now persist through all pages while you are logged on.
    • "Display" will take you back to this current page from anywhere.
    • To upload a photo, click "Upload" which will redirect you to the "Upload page".
    • For statistical analysis, you will click the "Analysis" button which will redirect you to the "Analysis Page".
    • At anytime, when you feel like you are done looking at photos, press "Logout" which will redirect you to a "Logout Page" which is essentailly a home page with the message of succesfully logged out.
